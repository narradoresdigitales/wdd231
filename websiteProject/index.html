About Me

Hi, I am Virginia Venturi, and I’m passionate about capturing the beauty of nature through the soft, delicate medium of pastels. My work is inspired by the intricate details and vibrant colors of the natural world—from the gentle sway of grasses to the play of light in a serene landscape. Each piece I create aims to evoke a deep connection to the environment, bringing the tranquility and wonder of the outdoors into your home. Explore my gallery and experience nature through the lens of pastel art.
