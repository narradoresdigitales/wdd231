Purpose

Venturi Art aims to showcase a collection of artwork, provide insights into various art styles, and offer a platform for art enthusiasts to connect. The website will feature a gallery of pieces, artist profiles, and a blog with articles on art techniques and trends.

Target Audience

  • Scenario 1: How can an art enthusiast navigate through the website to discover new artists and their works?
  • Scenario 2: What features does the website provide for art students seeking resources, tutorials, or inspiration for their own projects?

Content Structure

  • Home Page: Introduction and navigation links to the various sections of the website.
  • Projects Page: A list of projects with descriptions and links to live demos or repositories.
  • About Page: A brief bio and an overview of my skills and education.
  • Contact Page: A form for visitors to reach out to me directly.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #008080 (Teal)
  • Secondary Color: #D3D3D3 (Light Gray)
  • Accent Color: #FFD700 (Gold)

Usage:

  • Teal: Header, footer, primary buttons.
  • Light Gray: Backgrounds, card elements.
  • Gold: Highlights, hover effects, featured elements.
